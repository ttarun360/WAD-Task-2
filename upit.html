Unified Payments Interface (UPI)

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a real-time payment system developed by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). It enables users to link multiple bank accounts to a single mobile application, merging several banking features, seamless fund routing, and merchant payments into one platform.

Key Features of UPI:

  • Instant Money Transfer
  • 24/7 Availability
  • Link Multiple Bank Accounts
  • QR Code Payments
  • Mobile Number or Virtual Payment Address (VPA) for Transactions
  • Secure and Encrypted Transactions

How to Use UPI:

  1. Download a UPI-enabled mobile app from your bank or a third-party provider.
  2. Register and link your bank account(s) to the app.
  3. Create a Virtual Payment Address (VPA) or use your mobile number linked to the bank account.
  4. Initiate transactions using VPA, mobile number, or QR code.
  5. Authenticate transactions using UPI PIN.
  6. Receive instant confirmation of the transaction.

UPI has revolutionized the way people in India make payments, offering a convenient and secure method for transferring funds and making purchases.
